Why This Matters

‚öñÔ∏è Privacy & Regulation

Cookies aren't programs, but their misuse for surveillance triggered global laws. GDPR Penalty: Up to ‚Ç¨20M or 4% of global turnover.

Regulation Jurisdiction Core Requirement
GDPR EU / EEA Explicit consent for non-essential cookies (2018).
CCPA / CPRA California, USA Right to know + Opt-out of data sale (2020/2023).
ePrivacy EU "Cookie Law" - Consent for electronic tracking.
Data Science Implication: If your model ingests user data derived from cookies, you MUST verify the data was collected lawfully.
