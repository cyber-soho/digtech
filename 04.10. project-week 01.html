The Digital Evolution

Day 1 establishes the historical context of our digital world. Use this section to trace the timeline of major technological milestones and clarify the often-confused terminology of the digital shift.

1.1 Evolution Timeline

70s

Personal Computing

Shift from mainframes to individual workstations. The democratization of computing power begins.

90s

World Wide Web

Information becomes globally accessible. Connectivity redefines business and communication.

00s

Mobile & Cloud

Computing becomes ubiquitous (smartphones) and infrastructure becomes a utility (AWS, Azure).

Now

Industry X.0

Cyber-physical systems, AI integration, and total automation. The intelligent edge.

1.2 Core Definitions

Digitization

The technical process of converting analogue information (paper, sound, light) into digital binary format (0s and 1s).

Example: Scanning a paper invoice into a PDF.
